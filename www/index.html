Meet the jurors of the Goodman retrial

Selected over four days in Tampa, the six jurors and four alternates from Hillsborough County remain unidentified but answers they gave on a jury questionnaire provides a glimpse into their background. They are not allowed access to media accounts during the trial and they are being housed on the public’s dime at an undisclosed Palm Beach County hotel.
